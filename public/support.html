Your progress is automatically tracked as you explore skills and mark items as saved or completed. Visit your Dashboard to see detailed analytics, progress charts, and learning insights.

Key features:

  • Visual progress charts and statistics
  • Saved skills and bookmarked content
  • Learning streaks and achievements
  • Export/import your progress data

You can save skills by clicking the Save Skill button on any skill card. Saved skills appear in your My Learning page where you can:

  • View all your saved skills in one place
  • Organize skills by categories and priorities
  • Track your learning progress for each skill
  • Share your achievements with the community

Our trending score is calculated using the formula:

trendScore = popularity + views + growth

Components:

  • Popularity: Based on community engagement and adoption
  • Views: How many people are exploring this skill
  • Growth: Recent increase in interest and demand

This helps you discover the most relevant and in-demand skills in the current market.
